About

I started learning music when I was six. After playing and practicing for a little over five years, I began performance in 2012. Although I mainly play piano, I can play guitar, bass, and also sing.

In 2015, I began to practice video editing. I have worked on many video projects for organizations such as Faith Evangelistic Ministry (2015-today), Seasoned Woman (2016-2017), Tuombeni Kenya (2018), and the Global Missions Health Conference (2017).

I have been homeschooled for most of my life. Ever since I started interacting heavily with computers to edit videos, coding really interested me. At first, it was just learning how to use expressions in Adobe After Effects. Then Python took my fancy. When I saw that programming gave me the power to create nearly anything, I joined Moringa School in 2020 to study software development.

Adopt a Puppy

The aim of this project was to raise awareness of street animals. An estimated 750 million dogs are born without owners, most of which end up on the street. As one can imagine, that's a lot of dogs for animal services to keep up with! Adopt a Puppy was built to help solve this problem. If people adopted more puppies off the streets, the risks that come with free-ranging canines would be reduced significantly. This simple website was built with HTML and CSS.
